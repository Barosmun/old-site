Civilization V Mods

One of my favorite games of all time has to be Sid Meier's Civilization V. Not only is the standalone game great, but the community has added so much content that there are seemingly infinite ways to play. I've made several mods over the years, most which have gone unpublished. My recent endeavor was adding a new civilization to the game: Norway under Einar Gerhardsen.

The steam workshop page can be accessed here: Barrett's Norway

Spotify Lyrics Visualizer
