About Me

I was born on April 6, 2000. I graduated High School at 18, and will be graduating from College just before my 21st birthday. I live in Northeast Ohio, in the Akron-Canton metropolitan area.

I've had a fascination with programming and its applications since I was young and got my hands on the Scratch drag-and-drop language. In middle school, I started teaching myself to code. In high school, I took every Computer Science class I could, even opting to take several online courses from the nearby Stark State College. It was also around this time that I started making games with GM Studio. Once I started getting familiar with game development, I started participating in Ludum Dare 72-hour game development competitions, or "game jams". After high school, I attended the University of Denver pursuing a BS in Computer Science. Through academic pursuits and personal endeavors, I've expanded my knowledge and experience with programming logic, CS theory, and software development.

Now, quickly approaching my graduation date in March 2021, I'm eager to apply my experience to real-world problems in a professional environment.
