About Me

I was born on April 6, 2000. I graduated high school at 18, and college just before my 21st birthday. I live in Northeast Ohio, in the Akron-Canton metropolitan area.

I've had a fascination with programming and its applications since I was young and got my hands on the Scratch drag-and-drop language. In middle school, I started teaching myself to code. In high school, I took every Computer Science class I could, even opting to take several online courses from the nearby Stark State College. It was also around this time that I started making games with GM Studio. Once I started getting familiar with game development, I started participating in Ludum Dare 72-hour game development competitions, or "game jams". After high school, I attended the University of Denver graduating in March 2021 with a BS in Computer Science.

I currently work as a Full Stack Developer at MAGNI creating an online learning and quiz-taking application in partnership with our parent company THORS eLearning Solutions.
